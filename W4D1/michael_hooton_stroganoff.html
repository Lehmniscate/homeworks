Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef
  • 1 package of pasta
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 pound sliced mushrooms
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • salt and pepper (to taste)

Beef Stroganoff

Description

A dish of beef served in a creamy sauce containing sour cream on pasta

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil and add pasta
  2. Fry the onions and mushrooms in the vegetable oil and then set aside. Using the same pan, brown the beef and then drain the fat.
  3. Add sourcream and onions and mushrooms and broth and flour to the beef and let simmer
  4. When pasta is done and the sauce is thickened, serve warm
